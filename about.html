ABOUT



POLIVER, a squishing together of the names Polly and Oliver. These two happen to be the admirable authors of this blog, which tells of the London pair’s exciting journey around Japan. All photography and words come from the adventurous duo, who hope you enjoy reading about the wonderous things they’ve seen!
